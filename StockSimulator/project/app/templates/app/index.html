Simulate Stock Performance

A visualisation tool for your market insights
Define custom stocks and simulate their performance
Define events which impact your stock throughout the year
Create a custom portfolio using your stocks
Generate charts alongside your simulation

StockSimulator allows you to define custom stocks and simulate their performance over a year if they existed in a bubble. You can then define custom events which affect the stock allowing you to visualise how your insights might affect the market.

Disclaimer: StockSimulator does not predict the market but rather estimates how a stock might perform based on user defined attributes. It is primarily a visualisation tool and should not be used as a basis for financial decisions.
